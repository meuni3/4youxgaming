4YOUXGAMING STORY

4youxgaming is a group of people who love games and electronic sports, including online group games or other group games. The beginning of the idea is that we were searching during group games for players whose level matches or is close to the level in which we were playing, but we could not and did not find a site or application that provides all of these features. This idea came to our minds about this site, which is similar and matches all the characteristics that we were looking for. Our website aims to make friends in various games at a level that matches the level of the player, especially in the Arab community, and from here our launch of the idea of site ​​4youxgaming began.
